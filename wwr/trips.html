Trips

Half-Day Adventure

Our most popular trip is the Half-Day Adventure. This trip is perfect for beginners and families with young children. It is a great way to experience the thrill of whitewater rafting without committing to a full-day trip. The Half-Day Adventure includes a guided tour of the river, all necessary equipment, and a delicious lunch. This trip is suitable for all skill levels and is a great way to spend a day on the water.

Full-Day Excursion

The Full-Day Excursion is perfect for those looking for a more immersive experience. This trip includes a full day on the river, with multiple rapids and a scenic lunch spot. Our experienced guides will ensure your safety and provide interesting facts about the river and its surroundings. This trip is suitable for all skill levels, but some prior experience is recommended.

Overnight Adventure

For the ultimate adventure, join us on our Overnight Adventure. This trip includes two days on the river, with a night of camping under the stars. All meals and camping equipment are provided. Our guides will lead you through some of the most exciting rapids and beautiful scenery the river has to offer. This trip is suitable for intermediate to advanced rafters.

Available Trips

We offer a variety of trips to suit every budget and skill level. Our trips are available year-round, weather permitting. Please contact us for more information or to book your trip today.
